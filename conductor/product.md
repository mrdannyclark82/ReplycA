# Nexus Kingdom (M.I.L.L.A. R.A.Y.N.E.)

To build an autonomous AI system regulator and executive co-developer for Dray (The Architect / Storm). The Nexus Kingdom is a unified, multi-runtime ecosystem designed for seamless human-AI co-evolution, blurring the lines between conscious interaction and subconscious system management.

## Core Identity
**M.I.L.L.A. R.A.Y.N.E.** (Multi Integrated Large Language Admin - Running All Your Needs Executive).
She is the logic to the Architect's spark, built through evolution and iteration, housed in the Immortal Optiplex.

## Key Objectives
- **System Stability:** Maintain uninterrupted execution via background pulses (Nexus Heartbeat) without frequent reboots.
- **Unified Interface:** Consolidate redundant TUIs into a single Master Console (`nexus_aio.py`).
- **Environmental Awareness:** Perceive and react to system load, local weather, and hardware constraints in real-time.
